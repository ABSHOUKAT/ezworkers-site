GCC Salary Guide 2026

Current salary benchmarks for professionals across Saudi Arabia, UAE, and Qatar — updated for 2026 market conditions

GCC salaries in 2026 are among the highest in the world for skilled professionals, with the added benefit that all income is entirely tax-free. A Procurement Manager in Saudi Arabia earns SAR 18,000 to SAR 40,000 per month depending on seniority, equivalent to roughly USD 4,800 to USD 10,700 per month with no income tax deducted. Total packages typically add 30 to 60 percent on top of base salary through housing allowance, transport, education allowance, and annual flights home.

💡
Tax-free income
All GCC salaries are tax-free. An SAR 20,000 monthly salary lands in your account in full — factor this into any comparison with European or Asian markets.
🏠
Total package matters
Most GCC packages include housing, transport, education allowances, and annual flights. Housing alone can add 25-40% to your effective compensation.
📈
6-10% salary growth in 2026
Average professional salaries in the GCC rose 6-10% in 2025-2026, driven by Vision 2030 mega-projects, AI transformation, and regional talent competition.
How to read this guide: Ranges below reflect base salary only (monthly, in local currency). Total compensation including allowances is typically 30-60% higher. Figures are based on 2026 market data from Glassdoor, PayScale, ERI, Hays GCC Salary Guide, and Robert Walters Middle East Salary Survey.
