Privacy policy

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

When you use EzWorkers, we may collect your email address if you subscribe to job alerts, and your CV if you submit one. We also collect standard analytics data (pages visited, device type) via Google Analytics.

How we use it

  • To send you job alerts you have requested
  • To display your CV to employers for roles you apply to
  • To improve EzWorkers based on usage patterns

What we never do

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your CV without your consent
  • Send unsolicited marketing email

Data storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure hosted in the EU. We follow industry-standard security practices.

Contact

For any privacy questions, contact us at privacy@ezworkers.com
