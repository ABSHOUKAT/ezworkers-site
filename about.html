Connecting GCC talent with GCC opportunity

EzWorkers is the job search engine built specifically for professionals and employers across Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain and Oman

What we do

EzWorkers aggregates and publishes live job listings from across the Gulf Cooperation Council region, refreshed automatically every 6 hours. We focus on procurement, construction, engineering, oil and gas, finance, and IT roles, the sectors driving Vision 2030 and the GCC's broader economic diversification.

Job seekers build a single profile, generate a GCC-ready CV, and apply to roles with one click. Employers post jobs free, review applicants through a structured pipeline, and reach a region-specific audience without competing against irrelevant global listings.

Our mission

The Gulf region is undergoing the largest economic transformation in its history, driven by Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030, UAE's diversification strategy, and similar national programmes across Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain and Oman. That transformation needs skilled people moving efficiently to where they are needed. EzWorkers exists to make that connection faster, clearer, and fairer for both job seekers and employers.
